This site blossomed from the creative minds of Team Pixelators during the spirited Hack-N-Win hackathon at the Library of CGC Jhanjeri.

As we embarked on the journey of crafting this website, we were second-year (4th semester) students of Computer Science and Engineering at Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College, Ludhiana, Punjab. Comprising a dynamic team of four, our mission was clear: to empower students in self-discovery and guide them in exploring future career paths aligned with their passions. Central to our endeavor is a sophisticated chatbot, designed to intuitively assist students in uncovering their interests. Additionally, we've curated a self-exploration section, inviting students to delve into a myriad of courses.
